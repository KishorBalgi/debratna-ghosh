About Me

I am a self taught, published and exhibited street photographer, who aims at bringing out various flavors from different aspects of street as well nature. I chose Photography as my career path soon after the completion of my engineering degree out of the inner drive and determination towards turning out the passion into profession while nurturing it through more sound ways. Currently I am working with on my own project named ‘life on roads’, A collection of soul’s stories which emphasize to taste some intense and abandoned aspects of life, beside freelancing along with some mentoring and side ventures.
